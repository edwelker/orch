Free Senior Transportation

The Columbia Senior Transportation Service, funded by the Columbia Association, provides free door-to-door transportation to concerts and other events via URTA vans and mini-buses. Telephone your request 24 hours in advance of the concert. If you need a wheelchair lift, please mention this when you call. To request a ride or get information, please call 410-715-3087.
